<p><b>Wang Jianmin</b> (Chinese: <span lang="zh">王建民</span>; pinyin: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Wáng
            Jiànmín</span></i>; November 1942 – 28 September 2021) was a full general (<i>shangjiang</i>) of the
    People's Liberation Army (PLA) of China. He joined the PLA in June 1962, and the Chinese Communist Party in
    September 1963.
</p>

<h2>Career</h2>
<p>Wang was born in Luannan County, Hebei, in November 1942.
</p>
<ul>
    <li>1973, Operations office director and assistant section chief, operations section, operations department,
        Shenyang Military Region.</li>
    <li>1983, Assistant Department Chief, Operations Department.</li>
    <li>1985, Division Commander.</li>
    <li>1987, Department Chief, Operations Department, Shenyang Military Region Command.</li>
    <li>1989, Studied at the PLA National Defense University.</li>
    <li>1993, Deputy Commander, 23rd Group Army.</li>
    <li>1995, Commander, 23rd Group Army. Commander, 39th Group Army.</li>
    <li>1997, Candidate member, 15th National People's Congress.</li>
    <li>1999, Chief of Staff, Shenyang Military Region.</li>
    <li>2000, Deputy Commander, Shenyang Military Region.</li>
    <li>2002, Commander, Chengdu Military Region.</li>
</ul>
<p>He was a member of the 16th Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
</p>
<p>He attained the rank of major general in July 1994, lieutenant general in 2000, and full general on June 24, 2006.
</p>
<p>On 28 September 2021, he died from an illness in Chengdu, Sichuan, aged 78.</p>
